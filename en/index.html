2. Scope

This policy applies to the WasteLess application and related services (authentication, cloud sync, recipe suggestions, shopping list, notifications, and support).

It does not cover third-party services outside our control (for example, external pages opened from the app or Apple/Google Store pages).

3. Data we collect

We only collect data needed to provide and secure the service.

  • Account data: user identifier (UID), email address, and sign-in method (email, Google, Apple).
  • Functional data: food inventory, usage/disposal history, favorite recipes, shopping list, food preferences, and goals.
  • Technical data: push notification token (FCM), minimum security metadata, and limited technical logs for diagnostics and abuse prevention.
  • Content data: product details from barcode scanning (Open Food Facts) and recipe-related data generated through the backend proxy.
  • Support data: information you voluntarily send by email.

4. Device permissions

You can revoke permissions in your device settings. Some features may then be unavailable or degraded.

  • Camera: barcode scanning.
  • Photos/gallery (platform-dependent): profile image selection.
  • Notifications: expiration alerts and reminders, only if enabled on your device.

5. Purposes and legal bases

  • Providing WasteLess services (contract performance): authentication, cloud backup, inventory sync, recipe suggestions, planning, and shopping list features.
  • Sending notifications when enabled (consent / device choice).
  • Platform security (legitimate interest): abuse mitigation, infrastructure protection, and fraud prevention.
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.

6. Recipients and processors

Your data may be processed by the following service providers only for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging): hosting, authentication, database, server functions, notifications.
  • OpenAI: AI-generated features and, where applicable, recipe images through the backend proxy.
  • Spoonacular: recipe services for relevant product flows.
  • Open Food Facts: product data after barcode scans.
  • Resend: transactional email delivery (for example, welcome email if enabled).
  • Apple and Google: federated sign-in and in-app purchase/subscription management through app stores.

7. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside your country of residence, including outside Canada or outside the EEA.

Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as contractual commitments and technical/organizational security controls.

8. Retention

When your account is deleted, data is deleted or anonymized subject to technical and legal constraints.

  • Account and app data: kept while your account remains active.
  • Technical logs: retained for limited periods, proportionate to security and diagnostics needs.
  • Support emails: retained for as long as needed to handle your request and meet legal obligations.

9. Account deletion and erasure

You may request account deletion from the app (where available) or by contacting support.

Deletion triggers cleanup of associated data (profile, inventory, history, favorites, shopping lists, and related records), except where retention is required for legal, security, or anti-fraud reasons.

Important: deleting your app account does not automatically cancel store subscriptions. Subscription management remains in App Store/Google Play settings.

10. Your rights (Canada, France, EU depending on your location)

To exercise your rights, contact support.wasteless@gmail.com and include your request plus the email associated with your account. We may request reasonable identity verification.

  • Access, correction, and update rights.
  • Erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability where applicable.
  • Withdrawal of consent (for example, notifications) without retroactive effect.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

11. Security

No transmission or storage method is completely risk-free. We apply reasonable safeguards aligned with the sensitivity of data and service risks.

  • Access controls and least-privilege principles.
  • Secure communication channels (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Firebase security rules and server-side anti-abuse mechanisms.
  • Limited technical logging for diagnostics and security only.

12. Children

The service is not designed to intentionally collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.

If you believe a child has provided personal data inappropriately, contact us for prompt review and deletion.

13. Cookies and similar technologies

The mobile app does not use a traditional website cookie banner. Some technical components (for example, authentication sessions and cloud services) may rely on strictly necessary equivalent mechanisms.
