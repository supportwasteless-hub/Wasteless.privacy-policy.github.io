1. How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your WasteLess account from the app when an in-app deletion option is available.

If you cannot find that option or can no longer access the app, you can email support.wasteless@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.

2. Identity verification

To protect your account, we may request reasonable identity verification before deleting data.

3. Effect of deletion

Unless legal retention or security needs apply, account deletion results in deletion or anonymization of associated data, including profile, inventory, history, favorites, shopping lists, meal plans and certain account-linked technical records.

4. Subscription

Deleting your WasteLess account does not automatically cancel a subscription purchased through Apple App Store or Google Play.

You must cancel and manage your subscription from your Apple or Google account depending on your platform.
